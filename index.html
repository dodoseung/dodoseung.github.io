About me

Hi! I'm Seungwon Do. I am a researcher and developer. My research interest is a Human Computer Interaction (HCI). Now, I am a researcher in the Defence & Safety ICT Research Department, Intelligent Convergence Research Laboratory, ETRI, Korea. I received a master's degree from the Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST. I conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Byungjoo Lee at the Interactive Media Lab. I received a bachelor degree from the Electrical Engineering at POSTECH.

I have an interest to make a computational model using data extracted from an interaction between a user and system. My goal is to give an improved experience to a user. My current research subject is a voice interaction using the Natural Language Processing (NLP). If you wanna interact with me, feel free to contact me.
