About me

Hi! I'm Seungwon Do. I am a researcher and developer. My research interest is a Human Computer Interaction (HCI). Now, I am a researcher in the Defence & Safety ICT Research Department, Intelligent Convergence Research Laboratory, ETRI, Korea. I received a master's degree from the Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST, Korea. I conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Byungjoo Lee at the Interactive Media Lab. I received a Bachelor degree from the Electrical Engineering at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH).
